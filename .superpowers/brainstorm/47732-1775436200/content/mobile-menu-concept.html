B. Services Accordion with Numbered Sub-items

Tap "Services" to expand/collapse sub-links. Sub-items have numbered indices (01, 02, 03) and subtle blue-tinted backgrounds. Chevron rotates on expand. Also shows contact email at bottom.

Shared Features (Both Options)

  • Parallax push: Main content shifts left + scales to 0.92, creating depth
  • Structural watermark: Faint truss/grid SVG at ~4% opacity in background
  • Staggered entry: Each link fades in with translateX, 60ms apart
  • Smooth close: Drawer slides out, content pushes back — spring easing
  • Backdrop tap: Tapping the dimmed content area closes the menu
  • Larger, readable text: 20px nav links (up from 12px current)
  • Language switcher: Bottom of drawer, separated by hairline
