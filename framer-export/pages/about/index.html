Our Vision

To be a leading partner in structural engineering and real estate development, supporting the creation of large-scale, high-impact projects across Quebec.

Our Mission

To deliver optimized, value-engineered solutions that enhance project viability, reduce costs, and support efficient execution—from early-stage planning to construction.

Core Value

We prioritize clarity, efficiency and results-driven thinking—delivering solutions that align engineering performance with financial objectives and real-world construction constraints.

Beginnings

Engineering Expertise Driving Real Estate Development

Founded in 2022, Metanova Experts-Conseils brings together over 20 years of combined experience in structural engineering, real estate development and construction. The firm works alongside investors, developers and industry professionals to support the planning, structuring and execution of projects across Quebec.

Metanova is involved at every stage of a project, from site analysis and feasibility to design optimization and construction coordination. Our approach combines technical expertise with a development-driven perspective to deliver projects that are efficient, viable and aligned with market realities.

A Practical and Strategic Approach

Development-Oriented – We approach each mandate with a focus on project viability, optimization and long-term value.

Value Engineering – We integrate cost optimization strategies from the earliest stages of design.

Integrated Execution – We ensure coordination between all stakeholders to support efficient and predictable delivery.

Meet the executive team

Suddam Al-Salem, ing.

Managing Partner - Engineering Division

Suddam Al-Salem, ing.

Managing Partner - Engineering Division

Suddam Al-Salem is a structural engineer specializing in buildings, steel, wood and concrete. With several years' experience acquired with one of the most renowned companies in the field of structural engineering, NCK, Suddam Al-Salem has worked on projects of varying scope and complexity. As a project manager, he has developed comprehensive expertise in design, project management and site inspection, enabling him to master all the technical and organizational aspects of a project. This global vision of the profession enables Suddam Al-Salem to fully understand the challenges specific to each project, and to provide innovative and appropriate solutions. He combines technical rigor and creativity to design robust, efficient structures, while ensuring that the project blends harmoniously into its environment. Today, at the helm of Metanova Experts-Conseils, he puts his expertise at the service of his customers, whether for small projects or large-scale realizations, with one overriding priority: to provide a high-quality, personalized and efficient service, ensuring that each project is a technical and financial success.

Muhannad Al-Salem

Managing Partner – Real Estate Development Division

Muhannad Al-Salem

Managing Partner – Real Estate Development Division

With over a decade of experience in real estate development, Muhannad has played a key role in the planning, structuring and execution of major projects across Quebec. His background in architecture and construction management, combined with eight years of experience as a general contractor, provides him with a comprehensive understanding of the entire development lifecycle. Since 2011, he has worked closely with municipalities, leading zoning change processes and securing project approvals, while collaborating with investors to structure financially viable, high-impact developments. His expertise lies in navigating regulatory frameworks, coordinating with key stakeholders, and aligning each project with market dynamics, investment strategies and long-term community growth.
