About us

Where structural expertise meets strategic development.

Our Vision

To be a leading partner in structural engineering and real estate development, supporting the creation of large-scale, high-impact projects across Quebec.

Our Mission

To deliver optimized, value-engineered solutions that enhance project viability, reduce costs, and support efficient execution, from early-stage planning to construction.

Core Value

We prioritize clarity, efficiency and results-driven thinking—delivering solutions that align engineering performance with financial objectives and real-world construction constraints.

At Metanova, structure engineering is a strategic tool. We bring clarity to complex projects, reduce risk, and ensure each development is built on a solid, optimized foundation.

We combine technical expertise, strategic planning and collaboration to maximize project efficiency and long-term value.

We work alongside developers, investors and contractors to unlock site potential and deliver high-performing, value-engineered projects—on time and within budget.
