We lead residential and mixed-use developments through every critical phase — strategic planning, zoning approvals, infrastructure coordination, and construction oversight — ensuring projects remain financially controlled, regulatory-compliant, and aligned with development objectives. Our role is to reduce uncertainty, protect capital, and maintain momentum from early planning through final delivery. Download Brochure Download What’s include in the services? Strategic Planning & Feasibility Analysis – We assess project viability, land-use constraints, infrastructure requirements, and regulatory considerations to establish a clear and achievable development roadmap. Zoning Amendments & Municipal Approvals – We manage zoning changes, approval processes, and coordination with municipalities to streamline regulatory pathways and prevent costly delays. Budgeting & Cost Control – Our team develops realistic financial projections and monitors cost structures to protect margins and ensure responsible resource allocation. Scheduling & Milestone Management – We create structured timelines, track progress, and coordinate stakeholders to maintain momentum and avoid disruption. Infrastructure & Contractor Coordination – We oversee infrastructure works and coordinate contractors to ensure technical alignment and execution quality. Risk Identification & Mitigation – We proactively assess technical, regulatory, and financial risks, implementing structured mitigation strategies to prevent setbacks. Construction Oversight – We monitor on-site progress, maintain quality standards, and ensure adherence to approved plans and engineering requirements. Stakeholder Coordination & Communication – We act as the central coordination point between developers, municipalities, consultants, engineers, and contractors. At Metanova our project management and consulting services are designed to turn vision into reality with precision, expertise, and unwavering commitment to excellence.
