Our Vision To be a leading partner in structural engineering and real estate development, supporting the creation of large-scale, high-impact projects across Quebec. Our Mission To deliver optimized, value-engineered solutions that enhance project viability, reduce costs, and support efficient execution, from early-stage planning to construction. Core Value We prioritize clarity, efficiency and results-driven thinking—delivering solutions that align engineering performance with financial objectives and real-world construction constraints. At Metanova, structure engineering is a strategic tool. We bring clarity to complex projects, reduce risk, and ensure each development is built on a solid, optimized foundation.
